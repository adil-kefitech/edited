QuestionPaper.qp_code  (e.g. "V5796")
       ↓ (via QuestionPaperExamMapper.qp)
Examination  (the actual exam this QP belongs to)
       ↓ (via ExaminationSchedule.exam + programme_semester_course filter)
ExaminationSchedule  (specific course schedule under that exam)
       ↓ (via CampScheduleCourse.exam_schedule)
CampScheduleCourse  (links a camp schedule to an exam schedule)
       ↓ (via CampScheduleCourse.camp_schedule)
CampSchedule  (the specific camp schedule — has start_date, end_date, status)
       ↓ (via CampRoleAllocation.camp_schedule)
CampRoleAllocation  (the officer/examiner allocated to this camp)
       ↓ (via CampSchedule.sub_camp → SubCampMaster.camp)
CampMaster  (the physical camp)
